- [IndieAuth](https://indieweb.org/IndieAuth): OAuth2 with your own domain name as a username
  + add `rel="me"` to homepage links to delegate auth e.g. `<a href="https://github.com/casperdcl" rel="me">GitHub</a>`
  + can self-host, or use services like <https://indielogin.com>
- [Webmentions](https://indieweb.org/Webmention): `@mention` equivalent for web pages
  + [example](https://aaronparecki.com/2018/06/30/11/your-first-webmention)
